# Background and Overview

LastMile is a store that sells office supplies and furnishings nationwide across the United States. The company has significant amounts of data in sales. This project analyzes and synthesizes this data in order to uncover critical insights that will provide LastMile's commercial success.

Insights and recommendations are provided on the following key areas:

- Sales Trend Analysis: Evaluation of sales trends 
- Product Level Performance: an analysis of LastMiles various products, understanding their impact on sales and profit
- Regional Comparisons: an evaluation of sales and orders by region

An interactive Tableau dashboard can be downloaded [here](https://public.tableau.com/views/LastMileStoreSalesDashboard/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)

# Executive Summary

## Overview of Findings

The LastMile Store Sales Analysis shows total sales of $733,215 and profit of $93,439 across 693 customers. Sales performance varies by region and product category, with strong concentration in a few key states and high-value items. California leads all states $146,388, followed by New York with $93,923, and Washington with $65,540, while several states, such as Alaska, Hawaii, Maine, and Wyoming, recorded $0 in sales. Product categories also show large differences, with phones generating $105,341 while the lowest category, Fasteners, generated only $858. These findings highlight where the company is performing well and where growth opportunities exist.
