# Background and Overview

LastMile is a local supermarket that sells office supplies and furnishings nationwide across the United States. The company has significant amounts of data in sales. This project analyzes and synthesizes this data in order to uncover critical insights that will provide LastMile's commercial success.

Insights and recommendations are provided on the following key areas:

- Sales Trend Analysis: Evaluation of sales trends 
- Product Level Performance: an analysis of LastMiles various products, understanding their impact on sales and profit
- Regional Comparisons: an evaluation of sales and orders by region

An interactive Tableau dashboard can be downloaded
